Welcome to Paris

Introduction

Paris, the capital of France, is known worldwide as the "City of Light." It is famous for its art, culture, fashion, and landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and Louvre Museum.

Popular Attractions

Eiffel Tower

Iconic tower offering stunning views of Paris.

Louvre Museum

World’s largest art museum, home to the Mona Lisa.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Gothic masterpiece located on the Île de la Cité.

Restaurants & Cafés

  • Le Meurice: Fine dining, French cuisine, 5-star rating.
  • Café de Flore: Historic café famous for pastries and coffee.
  • L’Atelier de Joël Robuchon: Creative French dining near Champs-Élysées.

Things To Do

Relax at Jardin du Luxembourg, shop along Champs-Élysées, or enjoy a cabaret show at Moulin Rouge.

Local Events

  • Bastille Day: July 14th celebrations with fireworks.
  • Paris Fashion Week: International fashion showcase.
  • Nuit Blanche: Annual all-night art festival.
